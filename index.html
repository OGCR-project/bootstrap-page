OUR CONSORTIUM

our priorities

Empowering land managers and local actors

We believe the transition to sustainable land use must be driven by those who know the land best. OGCR equips farmers, foresters, and communities with transparent data and practical tools to measure, monitor, and improve their carbon performanceâ€”ensuring that decisions are locally meaningful and globally impactful.

    Promoting intergenerational equity

    Climate solutions must be designed not just for today, but for tomorrow. OGCR is committed to long-term resilience, making sure carbon benefits are sustained across generations, so that both current and future land stewards share fairly in environmental and economic rewards.

    Facilitating equitable access to carbon and ecosystem markets

    We aim to break down barriers to participation by ensuring that carbon and ecosystem credits are accessible to all landholders, regardless of size, region, or resources. By creating an open, standardized, and transparent registry, OGCR helps level the playing field in the emerging carbon economy.

    OUR MISSION

    The Intergenerational Open Geospatial Carbon Registry (OGCR) is driven by the mission to provide transparent, scientifically validated, and openly accessible geospatial carbon accounting tools that enable fair recognition and reward for landholdersâ€™ contributions to climate action. Our vision is to empower stakeholders across Europe and beyondâ€”farmers, policymakers, researchers, and communitiesâ€”by creating a trusted, open-source framework that connects environmental responsibility with economic opportunity, ensuring carbon benefits are shared across generations. 

    how

    To achieve this, OGCR is building updateable geospatial baselines for soil, biomass, and peat across the EU, developing a hybrid modelling framework to evaluate management changes within CAP and CRCF initiatives, and creating a globally applicable carbon registry that integrates seamlessly with both voluntary and mandatory markets.
